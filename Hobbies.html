A Peek into My Hobbies

As an Israeli computer science student, my free time is a mix of adrenaline and relaxation. Flying drones lets me capture the world from above, and there's nothing quite like the thrill of a Barcelona football match. I keep active with running and calisthenics, pushing my limits physically. When it's time to unwind, you'll find me lost in the world of cinema, especially the Batman trilogy, which never gets old. Additionally, playing the drums allows me to express myself through music, adding rhythm to my life. These hobbies keep me balanced, blending my love for technology, sports, and creative expression.
