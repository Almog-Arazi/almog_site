💚 "Greenies" Platform

The first Upcycling culture promotion platform in Israel and the world.

What is Upcycling?

Upcycling is the process of taking something no longer in use and giving it a new life and function. Most people are not aware of the varied possibilities of Upcycling, spending many hours searching for ideas. This is discouraging, and usually, because of the lack of accessible information, people just give up...

Our Solution

An ecosystem - a growing community of Greenies that introduces the public to the Upcycling concept and allows environmental enthusiasts to dive deeper into the matter.
